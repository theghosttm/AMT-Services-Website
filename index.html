How It Works

AMT Console GUI is built for creators who want full control without unnecessary complexity. Our system operates through a structured command console, allowing precise execution of admin actions, feature toggles, and integrations directly within your Gorilla Tag setup.

Unlike traditional menus that rely solely on buttons, our console-based approach provides flexibility, speed, and expandability. Commands can be customized, extended, and mapped to your project’s specific needs.
